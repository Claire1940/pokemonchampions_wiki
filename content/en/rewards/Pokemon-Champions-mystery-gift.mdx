## Claiming Rewards from the Mailbox

A common point of confusion for new trainers is where the gift goes after the code is successfully entered. Unlike traditional RPGs where items appear in your inventory instantly, Pokemon Champions utilizes a Mailbox system to prevent inventory overflow and allow players to choose when they want to activate their rewards.

### Steps to Retrieve Your Gift:
1. Exit the Mystery Gift menu and return to the main Sub-menu.
2. Locate the **Mailbox** icon (usually indicated by an envelope).
3. Look for a message titled **"Special Campaign Gift."**
4. Open the message to see the details of the gift (e.g., Machamp).
5. Select "Claim" to move the Pokemon to your Box or your Party.

> **💡 Tip:** After claiming your reward, it is good practice to delete the message from your mailbox to keep your inbox organized and ensure you don't miss future notifications.

## Troubleshooting Redemption Issues

If you encounter an error while trying to claim a **Pokemon Champions mystery gift**, there are several common factors to check. The game's servers can occasionally become congested during peak hours, especially when a new code is announced on social media.

1. **Incorrect Code Entry:** Codes are often case-sensitive or require specific alphanumeric characters. Double-check that you haven't swapped a "0" (zero) for an "O" (letter).
2. **Expired Codes:** Most codes have a hard expiration date. If the system says "Code expired," the campaign has officially ended.
3. **Region Locking:** While most 2026 codes are global, some are tied to specific physical events in certain countries.
4. **Already Redeemed:** Each **Pokemon Champions mystery gift** code can typically only be used once per save file.
