Beyond the limited-time online competitions, Pokemon Champions features a robust "Month of Champions" celebration. This includes daily login bonuses and specific "Starter Missions" that act as an extended tutorial, rewarding you for exploring the game's deep customization features.

To maintain a steady flow of VP and Quick Coupons, you should incorporate a daily routine. The game rewards consistency, providing enough currency to fully train a new Pokemon every few days just through regular play.

## Recruitment Mechanics: Trial vs. Permanent

The way you "catch" Pokemon in this game is through the Recruitment feature. Every 22 hours, your pool of 10 available Pokemon refreshes. Using the **Pokemon Champions event rewards 2026**, specifically the 100 Quick Coupons from the Warm Up Challenge, you can bypass this timer 100 times to hunt for specific species or even Shinies.

### Trial Recruitment
Trial recruitment is free and lasts for 7 days. This is an excellent way to test a Pokemon in your team composition before committing resources. Note that Trial Pokemon cannot have their moves or stats altered.

### Permanent Recruitment
If you find a Pokemon you want to keep forever, it costs 2,500 VP or one Teammate Ticket. Once permanent, you can retrain the Pokemon as many times as you like. We recommend saving your permanent recruitments for rare species or those with "Marks" and "Shiny" status, as these are rarer in the daily pools.

## FAQ

**Q: How do I claim the free Gardevoir from the Pokemon Champions event rewards 2026?**

A: You must enter the "Warm Up Challenge" under Online Competitions and complete at least three full matches. Once the event ends on April 13, 2026, the rewards will be distributed to your gift box after the results are tallied.

**Q: Can I transfer the event rewards to Pokemon Home?**

A: No. Pokemon obtained directly through gifts, recruitment, or the Battle Pass in Pokemon Champions are locked to the game. However, Pokemon you bring *into* Champions from Home can be moved back and forth freely.

**Q: What is the fastest way to earn VP for the 2026 season?**

A: The most efficient method is to complete your daily login, perform one recruitment, and play one ranked match every day. Additionally, focusing on Weekly Missions can net you several thousand VP, which is enough to fully optimize multiple Pokemon.

**Q: Are there microtransactions to buy more rewards?**

A: Pokemon Champions does not allow you to buy the main currency (VP) with real money. You can purchase a Battle Pass or a Membership for extra box space and exclusive missions, but competitive power must be earned through gameplay.
