## How to Redeem Codes in Pokemon Champions

Redeeming these **Pokemon Champions free pokemon codes** is a straightforward process, but the menu layout can be slightly confusing for those used to the traditional mainline games. The Mystery Gift function is tucked away within the sub-menus rather than being on the main start screen.

1.  **Open the Sub-Menu:** Press the **X button** (or tap the menu icon on mobile) to bring up the primary navigation hub.
2.  **Navigate to Mystery Gifts:** Scroll down to the bottom section of the menu and select the "Mystery Gifts" icon.
3.  **Enter Password:** Select "Enter Password/Code" and input the desired code using the on-screen keyboard.
4.  **Check Your Mailbox:** Unlike previous games where the Pokemon is added directly to your party, Champions sends gifts to your **In-Game Mailbox**.
5.  **Claim Reward:** Open the Mailbox (found in the same sub-menu) and click "Claim" on the Special Campaign Gift to add the Pokemon to your roster.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Your roster has a maximum capacity (starting at 35 slots). If your "ranch" is full, you must return a Pokemon to the recruitment center before you can claim a new Mystery Gift.
