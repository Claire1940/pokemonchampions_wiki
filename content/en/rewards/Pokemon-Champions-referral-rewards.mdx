## How to Claim Your Rewards

Claiming your **Pokemon Champions referral rewards** is a straightforward process, but it requires manual synchronization in the 2026 client.

1.  Open the **Trainer Menu** and select the **Social** tab.
2.  Click on **Referral Status** to see your current recruit count and their progress.
3.  If a milestone is highlighted in gold, click **Claim Reward**.
4.  Items will be sent to your **In-Game Mailbox**, while digital art and skins will be added to your **Collection Gallery**.
