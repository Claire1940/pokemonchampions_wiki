## Advanced Tips for Victory Points and Progression

Your **Pokemon Champions login bonus** is just the beginning. To maintain a high-tier roster, you need to farm Victory Points (VP). VP is the primary currency used for permanent recruitment of Pokémon and purchasing items like Choice Scarfs or Life Orbs from the shop.

*   **Daily Missions:** Check your missions tab every day. These often reward 50-100 VP for simple tasks like "Use a Super Effective Move" or "Win 1 Casual Battle."
*   **Battle Pass Progression:** Even the free version of the Battle Pass provides Mega Stones and recruitment items. Ensure you are completing seasonal challenges to move through the tiers.
*   **Cross-Platform Sync:** By linking your account to [the official Pokémon site](https://www.pokemon.com), you can earn rewards on your mobile device during your commute and have them waiting for you on your Switch at home.

## Step-by-Step: Claiming Your Rewards

Follow this checklist to ensure no **Pokemon Champions login bonus** is left behind:

1.  **Login Daily:** Even if you don't plan to play, logging in triggers the daily VP reward.
2.  **Check the Mailbox:** Rewards from online competitions (like the Gardevoir event) are delivered here, not automatically added to your inventory.
3.  **Link Pokémon Home:** This allows you to bring in certain Pokémon from past games, which can save you thousands of Victory Points in recruitment costs.
4.  **Monitor the Shop:** Some items that appear in the Premium Battle Pass can occasionally be purchased with VP in the rotating shop.

## FAQ

**Q: Does the Pokemon Champions login bonus expire?**

A: Yes, most bonuses have expiration dates. The Dragonite Early Download bonus expires on August 31, 2026, while event-specific rewards like Gardevoir have much shorter windows, often ending within a week of the game's launch or event start.

**Q: Do I need a Nintendo Switch Online subscription to get the bonuses?**

A: While you can claim the login bonuses without a subscription, you will need an active Nintendo Switch Online membership to participate in the "Online Competitions" required for event-based rewards like the Gardevoir package.

**Q: Can I get Mega Stones without paying for the Premium Battle Pass?**

A: Yes. The developers have confirmed that Mega Stones can be purchased using Victory Points (VP) in the in-game shop, though they may require a significant amount of grinding compared to the Battle Pass route.

**Q: What are Quick Coupons used for in Pokémon Champions?**

A: Quick Coupons (also referred to as Speed Tickets) are used to instantly complete certain training regimens or skip the wait times for specific resource-gathering missions, allowing you to level up your Pokémon faster.
