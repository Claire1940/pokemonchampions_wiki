## Recruiting Your Pokémon in Pokémon Champions

Once you have flagged your Pokémon for a visit in HOME, you must manually "collect" them within the Pokémon Champions game. They will not automatically appear in your party or boxes until this step is completed.

### The Recruitment Menu

Navigate to the **Recruit** tab located on the bottom navigation bar of the main menu. From here, select the option labeled **"Collect from Pokémon HOME."** The game will perform a server check to identify any Pokémon currently waiting in the "Visiting" queue.

Upon collecting a Pokémon for the first time, the game will calculate its strength. It is important to note that Pokémon Champions uses a modified stat system. While Effort Values (EVs) and Individual Values (IVs) from the mainline games are recognized, they are scaled to fit the Champions balance.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Any training or level gains achieved in Pokémon Champions will stay within that game. If you return the Pokémon to HOME, these specific "Champions Stats" are saved on the server but will not alter the Pokémon's original stats in games like *Scarlet* or *Violet*.

## Returning Pokémon to Pokémon HOME

The "Visiting" mechanic is entirely reversible and, notably, **free of charge**. You do not need to spend VP or BP to send your Pokémon back to HOME.

1.  Open the **Recruit** menu in Pokémon Champions.
2.  Select **"Send back to Pokémon HOME."**
3.  Choose the specific Pokémon you wish to return and press the **X button**.
4.  Confirm the prompt. The Pokémon will immediately leave your Champions boxes and its "locked" status in HOME will be removed.

If you decide to bring the same Pokémon back to Champions at a later date, the game will remember its previous training progress, provided the Pokémon has not changed forms (such as through Mega Evolution or Terastallization changes in other games).

## FAQ

**Q: Does the pokemon champions pokemon home transfer cost any money?**

A: No, the transfer service itself is free. While Pokémon HOME has a premium subscription for increased storage, the act of moving Pokémon between HOME and Champions does not require any in-game currency like BP or VP.

**Q: Can I transfer Pokémon from Pokémon GO directly to Pokémon Champions?**

A: Not directly. You must first transfer your Pokémon from GO to Pokémon HOME. Once they are in a HOME box, you can then use the recruitment process to bring them into Pokémon Champions.

**Q: What happens to my Pokémon's items during the transfer?**

A: Items cannot be transferred through Pokémon HOME. Any held items will be automatically returned to your bag in the original game or removed if the Pokémon is coming from HOME's internal storage. You must equip new items within Pokémon Champions.

**Q: Why can't I see my Pokémon in the "Collect" menu?**

A: Ensure you have saved your progress in the Pokémon HOME app after moving the Pokémon to the Champions "Visiting" slot. If the app is closed without saving, the transfer will not be recognized by the Champions servers.
