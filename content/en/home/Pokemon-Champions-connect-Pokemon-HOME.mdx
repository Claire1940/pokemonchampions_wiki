## Understanding the "Visiting" Mechanic

Unlike the one-way transfers seen in older generations, the **Pokemon Champions connect Pokemon HOME** system utilizes a "Visit" status. This is designed to maintain the integrity of your Pokemon's data across different game engines.

When a Pokemon visits *Champions*, it essentially creates a local instance of itself within that game. Any training or level-ups you perform within *Pokemon Champions* will be saved specifically for that game. If you send the Pokemon back to HOME and then bring it back to *Champions* later, those local improvements will still be there, provided the Pokemon hasn't changed forms (such as evolving in a different game).

However, training done in *Champions* **does not** carry back to *Pokemon HOME* or other mainline titles. This separation ensures that the unique balancing of the *Champions* arena doesn't break the traditional RPG mechanics of the core series.

## How to Return Pokemon to HOME

If you need your Pokemon back for a different game or simply want to clear your *Champions* roster, the return process is straightforward and, most importantly, free.

1.  Open the **Recruit** menu in *Pokemon Champions*.
2.  Select the option to **Send back to Pokemon HOME**.
3.  Choose the specific Pokemon from your box and press **X**.
4.  Confirm the transfer. The Pokemon will immediately disappear from your *Champions* box and reappear in *HOME*.

Upon returning to *Pokemon HOME*, you will notice a special symbol on the Pokemon's summary page. This unique icon indicates the Pokemon has participated in *Pokemon Champions*, serving as a badge of honor for your competitive history. You can find more details on official competitive compatibility at the [official Pokemon website](https://www.pokemon.com).

## FAQ

**Q: Does it cost money to connect Pokemon Champions to Pokemon HOME?**

A: No, the connection and transfer process are completely free. You do not need to spend VP (Victory Points) to move Pokemon back and forth between the two applications.

**Q: Will my Pokemon lose its level when I transfer it?**

A: Your Pokemon's level is preserved, but its actual combat strength is recalculated based on the specific stat mechanics of *Pokemon Champions*. If you move it back to HOME, its original stats will remain as they were.

**Q: Can I transfer Pokemon from Pokemon GO directly to Champions?**

A: No. You must first move your Pokemon from *Pokemon GO* into *Pokemon HOME*. Once they are in a HOME box, you can then use the **Pokemon Champions connect Pokemon HOME** feature to bring them into the game.

**Q: What happens if I evolve my Pokemon in another game after it has visited Champions?**

A: If the Pokemon's form changes significantly, *Pokemon Champions* may treat it as a new entry. Any training data specifically saved in *Champions* for the previous form may no longer apply to the new evolved form.
