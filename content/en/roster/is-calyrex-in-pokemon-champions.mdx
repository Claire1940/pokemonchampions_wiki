## Major Mechanical Changes in 2026

The reason **is calyrex in pokemon champions** such a common question is due to the game's massive overhaul of traditional stats. If Calyrex were to be added today, its stats would function entirely differently than in previous generations. The 2026 engine has replaced the classic Individual Values (IVs) and Effort Values (EVs) with a simplified "Stat Point" (SP) system.

### Understanding the SP System
In this new title, all Pokemon are set to Level 50 automatically. The complexity of breeding for "Perfect IVs" has been removed, as every Pokemon effectively operates with perfect base stats for its level. Instead of the traditional 510 EV cap, players now distribute SP.

*   **1 SP = 1 Stat Point:** No more dividing by four to calculate your gains.
*   **Simplified Distribution:** You can now grant one extra stat point than was possible under the old EV system.
*   **Priority Brackets:** Mega Evolution and switching now occupy the same priority bracket, making the raw Speed stat the ultimate tie-breaker.
