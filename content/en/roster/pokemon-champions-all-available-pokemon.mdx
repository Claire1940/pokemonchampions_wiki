## The Final Form Roster Philosophy
The developers of Pokemon Champions have implemented a unique "Final Form" policy. To keep the competitive field focused and the asset quality high, the game largely excludes middle and base-stage monsters. For example, you will not find Bulbasaur or Ivysaur in the game files; instead, Venusaur serves as the representative for that line.

There are, however, notable exceptions to this rule. Certain monsters like Pikachu and Snorlax are included despite having evolutions because they possess unique G-Max forms or specific Z-Moves that are integral to the game's mechanics. This ensures that iconic mascots remain playable without cluttering the roster with hundreds of "NFEs" (Not Fully Evolved) creatures that rarely see competitive play.

The distribution of the **pokemon champions all available pokemon** roster is fairly even across the generations, though some regions received more "love" than others due to the presence of regional forms or recent DLC content.

## FAQ

**Q: Are all legendary Pokemon included in the pokemon champions all available pokemon list?**

A: No, the initial 2026 release of Pokemon Champions focuses on standard final evolutions. Most Legendaries and Mythicals are currently absent from the game files, though they may be added in future seasonal updates.

**Q: Why can't I find base-stage Pokemon like Charmander or Squirtle?**

A: Pokemon Champions uses a "Final Form" philosophy to streamline the competitive roster. Only the final evolutions (like Charizard and Blastoise) or monsters with unique battle gimmicks (like Pikachu) are included.

**Q: Does the game support Shiny Pokemon?**

A: Yes, every monster in the game has a corresponding shiny sprite. However, certain special forms, such as the Poke Ball pattern Vivillon or AZ's Floette, remain shiny-locked to maintain their rarity and lore accuracy.

**Q: How do I access Mega Evolutions and Z-Moves?**

A: These gimmicks are enabled via the Omni Ring. While the sprites for Mega Evolutions are in the game, you must obtain the specific Mega Stone or Z-Crystal within the game's progression system to use them in battle.
